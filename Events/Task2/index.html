Create a Weekly Salary calculator
Let the user enter his working hours from Monday – Sunday (6 input fields)
When clicking submit, calculate his salary:

  • A worker earns $10 an hour for the first 8 hours.
  • For every overtime hour, he earns $15.
  • On weekends, the employer pays double the usual rate. 10 hours worked on a weekday would pay 80+30 = $110, but on a weekend it would pay $220.
